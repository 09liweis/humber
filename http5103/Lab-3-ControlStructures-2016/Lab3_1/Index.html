Natasha Johnston

About Me!

Born into a rural community in Southern Ontario, Canada. Currently lives and works in Toronto. Natasha Johnston is a Canadian photojournalist and artist. She holds a BFA in Image Arts: Photography and a minor in Poltical Science from Ryerson University. Capturing the moments of peoples lives and the world around them is what is at the center of her work. She believes that photojournalism can contribute to social change. Throughout her travels she works with non profits to create dynamic virtual identies that can connect them to the global community. She continues to advance her skills in web development and design through contsant education. Her constant goal as a photojournalist is to tell the unseen stories that help us better connect as a society and global community.

Web Experience

Currently Natasha is working to become a Web Developer. Prior to her current education at Humber College, she explored the world of digital media through Interactive Documentaries. Using her final thesis at Ryerson University as a way to explore and research the different paths that can be taken to make an Interactive Documentary. She has very little experience in the coding world but has spent many hours trying to see what language would work for her. Even though she is just starting her web development journey, she will take her past work ethics and enthusiasm to make this opportunity a key part of her foundation.
